# 🎬 Netflix Content Strategy Analysis

## Project Overview

This project explores Netflix's global content library to understand its content strategy, distribution patterns, and production trends. Using Python for data analysis and visualization, the project investigates how Netflix allocates content across countries, genres, content types, and release periods.

The objective of this project was not only to practice data analysis skills but also to simulate the workflow of a real-world business analyst tasked with generating actionable insights from entertainment industry data.

---

## Project Objectives

The primary goals of this analysis were to:

* Analyze the distribution of Movies and TV Shows on Netflix.
* Identify the countries contributing the highest amount of content.
* Understand how Netflix's content library has evolved over time.
* Explore the most popular genres available on the platform.
* Investigate patterns in movie durations and content strategy.
* Practice end-to-end data analysis workflow using Python.

## 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis

The following business questions were explored:

### 1. Movies vs TV Shows Distribution

* What percentage of Netflix's catalog consists of movies versus TV shows?
* Does Netflix prioritize one content format over another?

### 2. Top Content Producing Countries

* Which countries contribute the most content to Netflix?
* How geographically diversified is Netflix's library?

### 3. Content Added Over Time

* How has Netflix's content acquisition strategy changed over the years?
* During which periods did Netflix experience major growth?

### 4. Most Popular Genres

* Which genres dominate Netflix's content library?
* What does this reveal about audience preferences?

### 5. Movie Duration Analysis

* What is the typical runtime of Netflix movies?
* Does Netflix favor shorter or longer content formats?

---

## 📈 Key Insights

Some major findings from this analysis include:

* Netflix's library is heavily dominated by movies compared to TV shows.
* The United States contributes the largest share of content, followed by several international markets.
* Netflix experienced significant expansion in content additions during its global growth phase.
* Certain genres consistently dominate the platform, indicating strong audience demand.
* Most movies fall within a relatively narrow runtime range, suggesting a standardized content strategy.

---

## 💡 Business Conclusions

Based on this analysis, several strategic observations can be made:

* Netflix follows a content diversification strategy while maintaining strong dominance in selected genres.
* International content production has become increasingly important to Netflix's global expansion.
* The platform appears to balance quantity with market-specific content localization.
* Content acquisition trends reflect aggressive growth strategies during expansion periods.
* Data-driven analysis can provide valuable insights into media platform strategy and consumer preferences.
